2. Q. Can an individual file suit against a organization if the government is actively pursuing the individual?

A. Not typically. If the government of other individuals have already started a case against an organization, it is unlikely that the individual will be able to be provided with a suit.

A. The guilty party could face serious charges, fines, imprisonment or probation. In some more serious cases, the party could receive more than one of these charges.
